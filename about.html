SHREE CONVENTIONS

SHREE CONVENTIONS is a landmark venue with the Guntur City’s largest and luxurious indoor Convention Centre. Located at M.G. Inner ring road which is major artery road of the city, the venue offers a choice of 3 venues

i.e. one Mega Convention hall named “Shree Vybavam” for guests, smaller convention halls of Different sizes named “Shree Utsav” & “Shree Elite”, 20 Rooms for Wedding Guests/Organizers Accommodation, and parking facilities.
